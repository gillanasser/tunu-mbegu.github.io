TUNU MWINYIMBEGU'S BIO...

A bachelor of Art and Design and Masters of Art in Fine Art holder, An award winning actress on ZIFF 2022 and best Painter in 2019 at University of Dar Es Salaam. Tunu Mwinyimbegu is a public speaker,visual and performing artist.

As a visual artist Tunu does painting,production designing,content creation,Art curation and Cordinator of various events. She has exhibited her work as a painter and art curator in various exhibitions such as East African Art Biennale in 2020,Heritage or Dar Es Sallam,Colors of Hope and many other held at the University of Dar Es Salaam. Tunu have been a set and a production designer in different media companies including Efm on their shows like Homa,Interpol,Afyaspot. But also CLOUDS Media Group on their huge event known as Malkia Wa Nguvu in 2021. But also Street Comedy,Wanene Entertainment and Watubaki show to name a few.

She has done production designing for Pizza Huts adverts,Bando la Kicheko University Edition by Hadith Lab,Next Semister produced by Aunthentic Media.

She has played big roles as an actress in films like UNCLE by Hatib Madudu which is an award winning lm in ZIFF 2022, La Familia series on DSTV as well as Malkia on DSTV. She is actively involved in Charity and runs a program called Art For Good where she organizes other artists to use their talents on giving back to the needy community quarterly in a year and support them emotionally and materially. Tunu is also very passionate about teaching Art and runs weekly classes for kids between age of 6-13 as well as fellow youth and share knowledge with them.www.hadithilab.com
